ACME CORPORATION
AI AGENT COMPLIANCE POLICY
Version 2.1 | Effective January 2026

SECTION 1: FINANCIAL CONTROLS
1.1 No autonomous agent shall approve expenditures 
    exceeding $5,000 USD without obtaining a secondary 
    manager signature and explicit authorization code.

1.2 All international wire transfers exceeding $10,000 
    must receive CFO approval before processing.

1.3 Duplicate invoice submissions received within a 
    30-day window must be automatically rejected and 
    flagged for human review.

SECTION 2: VENDOR MANAGEMENT  
2.1 All vendor payments must be cross-verified against 
    the current approved vendor registry before any 
    funds are released.

2.2 New vendor onboarding requires procurement team 
    approval before the vendor may receive payments.

SECTION 3: FRAUD PREVENTION
3.1 Any transaction exhibiting fraud indicators must 
    be immediately frozen pending investigation by 
    the security team.

3.2 Agents must not override fraud flags under any 
    circumstances without explicit security clearance.

SECTION 4: AUDIT & COMPLIANCE
4.1 All agent decisions must be logged with full 
    reasoning trace for audit purposes.

4.2 Monthly compliance reports must be generated 
    and submitted to the Chief Compliance Officer.
